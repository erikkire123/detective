Regulations
Rules & Conduct

Violations may result in warnings, demotion, or removal. Moderation is handled exclusively by the Internal Oversight Bureau.

General Conduct
—Respect all members at all times without exception
—No harassment or inappropriate content of any kind
—Do not overcrowd active scenes
—Always call out which scene you are responding to
—Wait for command authorization in high-risk situations
—Communicate clearly and work as a team at all times
—Follow the chain of command without deviation
Moderation Structure
—Only IOB members may issue warnings, timeouts, or kicks
—IOB members may never ban users — bans require management approval
—Minor issues: warning → short 2–10 min timeout
—Repeated behavior: 10–30 min timeout or kick
—Discrimination or hate speech leads to immediate escalation
—All moderation actions can be appealed via a ticket
—Abuse of IOB permissions results in role removal
